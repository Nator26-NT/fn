Financial Management job opportunities you can consider:


Bookkeeper Accountant Financial Manager Auditor Insurance Sales Manager Creditors Clerk


Bookkeeper

Bookkeepers manage a company’s transactions by recording all financial activities. They monitor the cash flow by documenting all the money that comes in and goes out.
To become a bookkeeper, you will need a N5 Financial Management Certificate. Salary according to Indeed R14 700 per month


Accountant

An accountant analyses the financial documents that the bookkeeper made. Accountants can then write reports which the organisation uses to keep track of their finances.
For this position you will need a N6 Financial Management Certificate.
Salary according to Indeed
R26 700 per month


Financial Assistant

financial assistant is responsible for all financial related administration. Their responsibilities include:
Organising financial statements and records
Maintaining payroll transactions
Assisting the financial manager
Knowledge of bookkeeping is an important skill to have, as well as the ability to work with finance related technology.
To become a financial assistant you need a N4 Financial Management Certificate.
Salary according to Indeed
R12 300 per month


Financial Manager

The purpose of this role is to supervise financial activities within the company.
Financial managers have to make sure that the financial department runs smoothly by overseeing the employees’ work.
If you don’t have a BCom degree, you can start with a N6 certificate and gain work experience, so that you can become a financial manager.
Salary according to Indeed R61 600 per month

Auditor As an auditor, you will have the responsibility to determine if a company’s books are accurate and ethical.
If you work as an internal auditor, you have to make sure that all the financial documents within your company are correct. External auditors do exactly the same, but they are hired by the company and do not work directly for them. In order to become an auditor you will need a N6 certificate. Salary according to Indeed R20 100 per month

Insurance Sales Manager

Insurance Sales Managers need to come up with strategies to reach the company’s target sales goal . They have to manage the employees’ work within the department to ensure that sales are progressing. A N5 Financial Management Certificate is needed for this position. Salary according to Indeed R50 900 per month

Creditors Clerk

A creditors clerk has many responsibilities, such as recording all transactions into a database and creating invoices for clients. Other tasks include: Managing phone calls Advising customers Working with a manager to create financial plans To become a creditors clerk you need to have a N4 Financial Management Certificate. Salary according to Indeed R15 600 per month
